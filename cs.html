Computer Science

Since its inception in 2003(AICTE - Approval Date:12/05/2003, Ref. No.: 06/01/MP/DEG/2003/006), the Department of Computer Science and Engineering has been continuously making progress in teaching and R&D activities.
Started with an intake of 60 students, the sanctioned intake was increased to 120 seats in 2006(AICTE Ref. No.: 06/01/MP/ENGG/2003/006) and further the intake was increased to 180 in the year 2012. In 2010, the department started a PG Program in “Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)” with an intake of 18 students and the intake increased to 36 in the year 2013.
In 2012, the department started one more PG Program in “Software System (SS)” with an intake of 18 students and the intake was increased to 36 in the year 2013.
Over the period, students with good score have got admission in the department.
A Large number of companies visit us for recruitment/ training. The Department has entered into an MoU with HCL,IBM, Microsoft, Oracle, Zensar Technologies, Hexaware Centre Of Excellence & GTT for software training and Faculty Development Programmes.
