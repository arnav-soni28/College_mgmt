Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is regarded as one of the oldest branches of engineering. The Civil Engineering Department is one of the revolutionary departments in the entire region. The department was established in 2009 with modern outlook of improving the standards of living of the society. The department since its inception has stood class apart in showcasing the talents of students and faculty as the department has bagged many awards in district, state as well as national levels. Apart from imparting education, mentorship and research assistance to U.G. and P.G. Students, the faculty of the department has also contributed significantly in providing testing of materials and consultancy service to local industries and state government departments.
