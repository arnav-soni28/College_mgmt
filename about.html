About

Gyan Ganga Institute of Technology and Science (GGITS) is a premier engineering college located in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, India. The college was established in the year 2006 and is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV) Bhopal.
GGITS offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering, management, and computer applications. The college has a state-of-the-art campus spread over an area of 25 acres, with well-equipped labs, libraries, and classrooms. The college has a diverse faculty of over 150 members, with expertise in various fields of engineering and technology.
GGITS has consistently been ranked among the top engineering colleges in Madhya Pradesh. The college has a strong focus on academic excellence, research, and innovation. The college has tie-ups with several industries and research organizations, providing students with practical exposure and training.
GGITS has an active student community, with several clubs and societies that organize various extracurricular activities such as sports, cultural events, and technical festivals. The college also provides ample opportunities for students to participate in national and international conferences, seminars, and competitions.
GGITS has a dedicated placement cell that works towards providing job opportunities to students. The placement cell has tie-ups with several reputed companies in various sectors such as IT, manufacturing, finance, and consulting. The college has a strong alumni network, with several successful graduates who have gone on to work for some of the top companies in the world.
Overall, Gyan Ganga Institute of Technology and Science is an excellent institution for those seeking a career in engineering, management, or computer applications. With its strong academic focus, state-of-the-art facilities, and vibrant student community, GGITS is a top choice for students from all over India.
